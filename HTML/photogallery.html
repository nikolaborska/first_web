Photogallery

These pictures are the result of my hobby: Digital paintings and drawings. Most of them are portraits of pets. But you can also see here cars, flowers, tattoo designs, etc.. I make paintings in two ways: Blurred/tidy or "Unmodified".

You can find the blurred image in the album "Animals", where there will be a picture of a Maine Coon cat. This cat that belongs to my husband's grandmother. In my opinion, this style of painting describes this breed of cat. They have a beautiful and soft coat. And the second style you can find in the album "Dog Alphabet" where it is used for each picture. Now you can compare which one you think is better and at the same time I have introduced you to my "style" of digital painting and drawing .... at least a little. I can't say if this is how my work will look like next year, as you know .. "life was like a box of chocolates. You never know what you're gonna get."
